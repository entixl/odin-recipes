Unbelievable Chicken

Descriptions

At first glance, this Unbelievable Chicken recipe might read like an odd mishmash of condiments and meat, but the unusual combination creates a surprisingly flavorful dish. I would argue that its success isn’t in spite of the “everything-but-the-kitchen-sink” approach—but rather because of it.learn more

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup cider vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons prepared coarse-ground mustard or to taste
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • ½ cup brown sugar or to taste
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt or to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Directions

  1. Mix cider vinegar, mustard, garlic, lime juice, lemon juice, brown sugar, salt, and pepper together in a large glass or ceramic bowl. Whisk in olive oil. Add chicken and toss evenly to coat. Cover, and marinate in the refrigerator for 8 hours to overnight.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
  3. Lightly oil the grill grate. Place chicken on the preheated grill, and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, until juices run clear. Discard any remaining marinade.
